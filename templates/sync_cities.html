Sync Cities

Reconciles AI nations' city lists against cities placed on the hex map. Cities found on a tile but missing from the nation page get added to the nation. Cities on the nation page that aren't placed on the map get placed using the AI's city placement logic. Existing cities and tiles are never modified or removed — only additions are made. Player-controlled nations are not affected.
