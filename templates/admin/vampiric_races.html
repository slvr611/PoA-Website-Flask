Vampiric Race Converter

Converts every pop in the selected nation to a "Vampiric" variant of its current race (e.g. an "Elf" pop becomes "Vampiric Elf"). If that race doesn't exist yet, it's created with the Undying positive trait, the Bloodthirsty negative trait, and the original race's preferred terrain. Founding nation is left blank.
